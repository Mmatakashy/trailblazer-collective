Our Story

Trailblazers Collective was founded by Davida Blanton in 2024, driven by a deep conviction that every person—especially the overlooked and underserved—deserves the chance to thrive. What began as Davida’s personal mission to walk alongside vulnerable youth quickly evolved into a grassroots movement...

What We Do

Our mission is to uplift vulnerable communities through love, action, and service.

  • Provide education for underprivileged children
  • Deliver food and health support to families
  • Empower women and youth through skills programs
