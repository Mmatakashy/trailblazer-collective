Our Story

Trailblazers Collective was founded by Davida Blanton in 2024, driven by a deep conviction that every person—especially the overlooked and underserved—deserves the chance to thrive. What began as Davida’s personal mission to walk alongside vulnerable youth quickly evolved into a grassroots movement...

What We Do

We implement principles of evangelistic community development and Kingdom business. By addressing physical needs while also fully integrating spiritual lessons, the people we serve have life-changing personal breakthroughs.

We see systemic poverty driving many problems for Kenyan communities—problems in physical, spiritual, and social wholistic health of individuals. We believe that communities can transform when they grow in education, skills, and healthy mindsets; and we effectively guide them in this.

Our mission is to uplift vulnerable communities through love, action, and service.

  • Provide education for underprivileged children
  • Deliver food and health support to families
  • Empower women and youth through skills programs

Women’s Outreach

We build relationships with Kenyan women who have suffered the harsh effects of trauma, abuse, and poverty. Many women are confused about what it means to be born again. They lack understanding about how to make disciples of Jesus Christ. Many want to be good mothers but have not had steady role models to teach them solid, Christ-centered parenting. By supporting them in health, hygiene, life skills, parenting, and sustainable income; we guide them in changing their mindsets and growing in confidence. Serving them in love yields measurable outcomes, bringing hope to them while opening doors for disciple-making

Business As Missions

Kenya’s poverty crisis is driven by numerous factors, such as government corruption, large-scale unemployment, and lack of knowledge among people groups. Many have not formed habits which contribute to a healthy lifestyle; and also lack understanding of God’s truth and love. Many Kenyans seek employment, microenterprise /entrepreneurship, or basic education; but still remain in poverty. They lack financial capital or knowledge to progress in ways that support their families well enough to break the cycle of poverty.

We believe that lasting change comes when people know how to use their own locally available, readily accessible resources to generate sustainable income for themselves and their families. We guide them in this, through income-generating activities and relational growth that builds spiritual maturity.

Community Health Evangelism

Poverty in Kenya causes many problems such as illiteracy, early pregnancy, transmission of infections, regular use of contaminated water and food (leading to severe illness and even death), alcoholism, divorce/separation of marriages (broken homes), and the spiritual effect of hopelessness leading to suicide, unresolved conflicts to murder or serious societal disputes. Spiritual forces working against Christ Jesus are hard at work in Kenyan villages. Witchcraft is prevalent and commonplace.

We work directly with individuals, families, local churches and other existing groups to lay foundations of faith and doctrine, physical health and hygiene lessons, and Bible study for practical application. Transformation comes by God’s Spirit when people overcome their physical and spiritual challenges.

Youth Empowerment

Young people in Kenya face great challenges and become stuck in cycles of addiction to alcohol, drugs, and sex. Many thousands want to be educated but cannot afford to attend school, which traps them in lifestyles of poverty, prostitution, and crime. By reaching out to youths of varying ages, Trailblazers Collective’s team is used of God to meet their needs. We impact vulnerable youths by training them in the way of Jesus and supporting them for a brighter future through education or skills training.
