Our Mission

We are on mission to improve the quality of life of impoverished communities, through education, Christ-centered mentorship, and income-generating activities.

Our Vision

Our vision is a self-sustaining, faith-driven, social enterprise that cultivates spiritual and physical health for people.

LeAnn Harris

Hi, my name is LeAnn, and I love Jesus. I came to faith at 30, realizing I was a sinner and that only Jesus could forgive my sins. Since then, my heart has been to love and encourage people, especially in Kenya, by sharing the saving grace of Christ. My desire is to glorify God, shine His light in dark places, and live by faith wherever He sends me. I hope to inspire the Kenyan people to embrace and walk in that same faith.

Silvia Eng'urant

Hi, I am Silvia Eng'urat. I love God, people, and serving communities from all walks of life. It is my joy to guide others spiritually in God’s Word and practically through education and employment. I am married with four children and serve in Friends North Quakers Church of East Africa. My training includes child evangelism, counseling, teaching, data collection, research, and computer operations. I hold a diploma in social work and community development and credentials in domestic violence and trauma recovery.
