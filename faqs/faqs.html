Programs

What is Community Health Evangelism (CHE Kenya)?
CHE equips local leaders to address poverty-driven challenges while integrating Bible study and evangelism for holistic community health.
What is ARMS Kenya?
ARMS provides Christ-centered support for women recovering from abuse through care groups, counseling referrals, and Scripture-based healing.
What is Business As Missions (BAM)?
BAM creates dignified jobs and discipleship opportunities by launching and mentoring ventures in rural Kenyan communities.
What is Youth Empowerment?
Youth Empowerment equips young people with leadership, entrepreneurship, and life skills to become Christ-like leaders.
Who can join your training programs?
Our programs are open to youth, women, and community members who are eager to learn practical skills and start income-generating activities. Selection may depend on available slots and community needs.
Do you charge fees for your training?
Some of our programs are subsidized through grants and donations, while others may require small contributions to cover materials and sustainability. Specific details are shared during enrollment.
Do you sell the products made at your tailoring hub?
Yes. We produce clothing and textiles for both individual customers and institutional clients. Purchasing from us directly supports our trainees and community programs.
